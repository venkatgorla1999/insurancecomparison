With coverage across the country, Bajaj Allianz policy holders will have the facility of availing spot assistance at the time of an accident or breakdown at any time and anywhere. Legal assistance services will also be provided during accidents along with flat tyre repair, replacement, car battery jumpstart and other such emergencies.


This add-on covers the cost of replacement of cars keys if they are lost or stolen. Apart from this, locks of the insured vehicle will also be repaired or replaced depending on the need.


In case of a serious bodily injury that may cause any kind of permanent disability, or an unfortunate event like death, compensation will be provided. This is also applicable for other passengers travelling in the car except the paid driver.


In case of damage to consumables such as the engine box, AC, gas oil, brake oil, power steering oil, engine oil, etc, the cost of replacement and repair will be covered by the insurance provider.



Policyholders whose damaged car may be undergoing repair or maintenance work at a network garage after an accident, will be eligible for this benefit. A daily cash allowance for conveyance will be provided.


The insured party will be protected if personal belongings lying in the car such as laptops, mobile phones etc are damaged or lost due to an unfortunate incident.


You can transfer up to 50% of your no claim bonus amount from any other car insurance provider.
